Heart Aid

Quantifying emotions to help therapists analyze marital issues

"Register positive event
of your marriage"
Prototype

Context

Project Brief
Final Graduation Project for my BA Design in 2010 Colombia. I wanted to make a tool to help people empathize with each other. I narrowed the context to married couples in couple‚Äôs counseling. The problem suggested a digital product as solution.

Problem
People would come to therapy to complain about their relationship. They usually tried to blame each other and tried to convince the therapist each was the good one. Therapy time was spent in complains leaving little time to actually work on the problems. Plus the tools to record progress of the therapy were all paper based.

User
The app is for young and tech-savvy married couples going to couple's counseling. They need a way to communicate their stories to the therapist. The web app is for the therapists. They need a clear visualization of the data received to find patterns and reach conclusions.

Solution
A mobile and web app that couples use to track negative/positive events and send to the therapist. Like a diary. I used graphic representations of emotions to help tell stories and quantify them. The app organizes the information in an interactive dashboard for the therapist's analysis.
