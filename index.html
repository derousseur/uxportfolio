Process

Each project has its own process. All of them start with my need to understand the context and the users. They all finish with a presentation to the client and documents for next steps.

1. Research

I need to understand the problem, the context, the audience and business expectations.

2. Ideation

I need to produce tools to help me analyze and brainstorm empathetic and strategic solutions.

3. Prototyping

I need to test my ideas, receive user feedback and improve them as early in the process as possible.

4. Presentation

I want to deliver work that my client feels satisfied with and that is useful for implementation.
