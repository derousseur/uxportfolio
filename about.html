I'm a UX designer based in San Francisco. I have a background in: research and strategy, visual design, and interaction design (hardware).

My goal is to use my work to improve how we interact with each other and the world around us.

The projects I like the most are related to social good, emotional connections, experimenting with new tech, travel and outdoors.
I look forward to meet a self-motivated team of designers that dream to work collaboratively (instead of competing) for big ideas, for good design and for a better future.

Process

Each project has its own process. But in general terms:


1. Research

I need to understand the problem, the context, the audience and business expectations.

2.1. Ideation

I need to produce tools to help me brainstorm and define empathetic and strategic solutions.

2.2. Prototyping

I need to test and validate my ideas, receive user feedback and improve them as early as possible.

3. Presentation

I want to deliver work that my client feels satisfied with and that is useful for implementation.
